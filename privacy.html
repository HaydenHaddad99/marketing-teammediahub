Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026  ·  Quiet Giant LLC d/b/a Team Media Hub

1. Overview

Team Media Hub ("we", "us", "our") is operated by Quiet Giant LLC. We are committed to protecting the privacy of coaches, parents, and all users of the Team Media Hub platform at app.teammediahub.co and this marketing website at teammediahub.co.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using our services, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information we collect

Team Media Hub collects only the information necessary to provide our service: your email address for authentication, team photos and videos you upload, and basic usage data to improve the platform.

Account information: Email address, display name, and a hashed password.

Media you upload: Photos and videos stored securely on AWS S3, accessible only to your team members.

Usage data: Pages visited, features used, and error logs — used only to improve the platform, never sold.

3. How we use your information

We use your data solely to operate Team Media Hub. Your photos and videos are private to your team — never shared publicly or used for advertising. We don't sell your data. Period.

  • To operate and improve Team Media Hub
  • To send important service notifications (account activity, updates)
  • To respond to support requests sent to support@teammediahub.co
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Media and team privacy

All teams are private by default. Only members who have joined a team with its unique team code can view that team's photos and videos. We do not make team content publicly searchable or accessible.

Coaches are responsible for managing who has access to their team. If you believe someone has joined your team improperly, contact us immediately at support@teammediahub.co.

5. Children's privacy

Team Media Hub is intended for coaches and parents/guardians of youth athletes. We do not knowingly create accounts for or collect personal information directly from children under 13. Media of minors may be uploaded by coaches and parents as part of normal team use — this content is private and accessible only to the team.

If you believe a child's information has been collected without parental consent, please contact us at support@teammediahub.co and we will promptly remove it.

6. Data sharing

We share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: We use trusted third-party services (e.g., cloud hosting on AWS) that process data on our behalf and are bound by data protection agreements.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of users or others.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, user data may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify users before any such transfer.

7. Data retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data and media will be deleted within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or accounting purposes.

8. Security

Media files are stored securely in AWS S3 with encryption at rest. Access is restricted to team members only via secure, time-limited URLs. All transfers use HTTPS. No system is 100% secure — please use a strong, unique password and contact us immediately if you notice unauthorized activity.

9. Your rights

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Team coaches can export or delete team media through their dashboard. To exercise other rights (access, correction), email us at support@teammediahub.co.

10. Cookies

We use essential cookies and local storage to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we'll notify you by email or by posting a prominent notice in the app. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent update.
