Common questions

Is it really free?
Yes — during early access, the full product is free to use with no credit card required. When paid plans launch, teams that joined during early access will get a discounted rate.
What happens when early access ends?
You'll be notified well in advance. Early access users will be offered a special pricing tier before we open to the public. We'll never surprise you with sudden charges.
How do parents join?
Coaches create a team and share a simple team code. Parents enter the code at app.teammediahub.co to join instantly — no account creation friction required.
Are there storage limits?
We're operating with generous limits during early access and working out the details for paid tiers. If you're hitting any limits, reach out and we'll work with you.
Do you have a plan for athletic directors or multiple teams?
Yes — that's exactly what the Pro Plan will cover. Contact us if you have a multi-team organization and want to talk through options early.
